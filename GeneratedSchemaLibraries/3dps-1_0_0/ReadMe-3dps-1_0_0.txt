OGC(r) 3D Portrayal Service (3DPS)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

The 3D Portrayal Service Standard is a geospatial 3D content delivery
implementation specification. It focuses on what is to be delivered in which
manner to enable interoperable 3D portrayal.

More information may be found at
 https://www.ogc.org/standards/3dp/

The most current schema are available at http://schemas.opengis.net/
and all OGC schemas may be downloaded in a complete bundle from
http://schemas.opengis.net/SCHEMAS_OPENGIS_NET.zip

* Latest version is: http://schemas.opengis.net/3dps/ *
